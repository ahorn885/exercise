### 5.4 Known Limits
AI systems can produce confident-sounding errors. Recommendations may be inconsistent across sessions, miss context, or fail to account for risks that a human coach would notice. ### 5.5 No Human Review of Every Output
AI outputs are generated automatically and are not individually reviewed by our staff before delivery. We review patterns of output and feedback to improve the system; we do not pre-approve every plan or session.
