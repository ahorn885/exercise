What you're consenting to when you connect a service — click to expand

When you connect a fitness provider, AIDSTATION will read data from your account in these categories:

  • Activity data — workouts, durations, distances, heart-rate streams, pace, power, elevation gain.
  • Wellness data — body weight, resting heart rate, recovery scores (where the provider supplies them).
  • Sleep data — nightly sleep duration and sleep-quality summaries (where the provider supplies them).
  • Account profile — your name + provider user id, used to map incoming webhooks back to your account.

The specific scopes granted are recorded each time you authorise a provider. Review or revoke any provider any time from the Data page; revoking stops new data from being pulled but leaves historical data intact.
